Sidebar

I have always wanted to create and ripple social impact through my work, and always thought that a good platform to share my thoughts and ideas, would be starting off with a website.

After many many years of saying I would but not doing anything (thanks Executive Dysfunctioning due to undiagnosed ASD) may I please happily & excitedly present to all of my lovely site visitors to Zo Blogg's Blog; A place where I share my latest ventures, thoughts, rambles, schemes and antics.
Expect everything from laughter to tears as I discuss everything from my day to day life, how much I love a good cuppa tea, music, tech, games, family, and what it’s like to be 26 year old Intern at an Art Gallery with a strong focus on technology and science.
My routes taken through life so far have, more often than not, been non-linear and a bit abstract than straightforward. I will likely often touch on the trials and tribulations of dabbling late in life with very different career paths; moving from Tech to Art, without a degree or formal schooling beyond GCSE's, apprenticeships, and generally just trying to make life work; keeping afloat with my mental health as I've grown up, and how I’ve dealt with things since my recent diagnosis of Autism in 2018.
